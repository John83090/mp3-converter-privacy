1. Introduction

This App works entirely locally on your device for all media conversion. It does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information of its own.

This Privacy Policy describes how MP3 Converter: Video to Audio ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our Android application. The App contains a banner advertisement served by Google AdMob, which may collect certain data as described below.

2. Information We Collect

No Personal Data by UsAdMob Banner Ad

We do not directly collect any personal information. However, our advertising partner Google AdMob may automatically collect the following data to serve the banner ad:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Approximate location (country/region level, based on IP address)
  • App usage and interaction data for ad performance measurement
  • IP address

The App itself does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or phone book data
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Browsing history or usage analytics sent to our servers
  • Any files or media you convert

All media conversion is performed entirely on your device using the FFmpeg library. No files, metadata, or user data are uploaded to any server by the App.

3. Permissions Used

The App requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

To access video files you select for conversion.

READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

To access audio files you select for conversion.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

To save converted output files to your device storage. (Used on Android 9 and below only.)

INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Used to check network connectivity and to load the Google AdMob banner advertisement. No personal conversion data is transmitted.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

To show conversion progress and completion notifications.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC

To keep conversions running reliably while the App is in the background.

WAKE_LOCK

To prevent the device from sleeping during active file conversion.

No permission is used by the App itself to collect, track, or share your personal data.

⚠ This App displays a banner advertisement powered by Google AdMob.

The App contains a single banner ad served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This includes:

  • Device advertising ID for ad targeting and frequency capping
  • General location (country/region) derived from IP address

Google AdMob's data collection and use is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

6. Data Storage

All files processed by the App are stored only on your device. We do not upload, sync, or back up your files to any remote server or cloud service.

Converted files are saved to your device local storage. You can delete them at any time using your device file manager. Any data collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google's own retention policies.
